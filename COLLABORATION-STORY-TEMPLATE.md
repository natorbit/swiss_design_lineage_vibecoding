## âœ… After AI Generates the Draft

AI will produce a comprehensive draft. **Your job is to refine it.**

### What to Check

**1. Authenticity**
- [ ] Does it sound like our actual process?
- [ ] Are specific details included (not generic)?
- [ ] Are the numbers accurate (time, iterations, scores)?

**2. Honesty**
- [ ] Does it mention struggles and mistakes?
- [ ] Does it show iteration (not just final success)?
- [ ] Does it include moments when you stopped AI or redirected?

**3. Fourth Wall Breaking**
- [ ] Does it speak directly to readers?
- [ ] Does it point out design elements they're experiencing?
- [ ] Is the tone engaging (not academic)?

**4. Balance**
- [ ] Does it show what YOU brought (vision, judgment, direction)?
- [ ] Does it show what AI brought (speed, expertise, execution)?
- [ ] Is it a true collaboration story (not "AI did everything")?

### What to Add

**AI can't know these thingsâ€”you must add them:**

**Personal Motivations:**
- Why you REALLY chose this style (not just academic reasons)
- What excited you about it
- What worried you about attempting it

**Specific Moments:**
- A breakthrough moment ("I realized X when...")
- A frustrating moment ("We struggled with Y for hours...")
- A surprising moment ("I didn't expect Z to be so...")

**Your Learning:**
- "I used to think [X] but now I understand [Y]"
- "The hardest part was..."
- "The most satisfying part was..."

**Gut Reactions:**
- "This felt wrong even though I couldn't explain why..."
- "When I saw the mobile version, I immediately knew..."

### How to Refine

**Prompt AI to expand sections:**
> "The 'Phase 4: Build & Iterate' section feels too brief. Expand it with more specific details about the design reviews we did. Include the hero section iteration where we went through 3 versions before it felt right."

**Prompt AI to add specifics:**
> "In the 'Design Decisions' section, add more about why we chose [specific choice]. Explain the trade-off we considered and why we went this direction."

**Add your voice directly:**
- Edit sections to include your personal thoughts
- Add anecdotes AI doesn't know about
- Inject your personality and style

**Instead of:**
> "We created a Neo-Brutalist design system."

**Write:**
> "See that thick black border around this paragraph? That's Neo-Brutalism in action. We went through three iterations on border thicknessâ€”first it was 2px (too subtle), then 6px (too aggressive), and finally 4px (just right). You're seeing the third version right now."

---

**Instead of:**
> "Typography was important to our design."

**Write:**
> "Look at this heading. It's in Arial, set at 48px, with line-height 1.2, and it's ALL CAPS. That's not an accident. Neo-Brutalism rejects refined typography. ---

**Instead of:**
> "We refined the hero section multiple times."

**Write:**
> "That hero section at the top of the homepage? We wrestled with it for four hours. First version was too polished (the human said 'this looks like a corporate site, not Brutalist'). Second version was too chaotic (I went overboard with misalignment). Third version lost readability (mobile was a mess). The fourth versionâ€”the one that's live nowâ€”finally balanced brutalist rawness with functional usability. That balance is HARD."
